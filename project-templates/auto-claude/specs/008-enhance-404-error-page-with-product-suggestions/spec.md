# Enhance 404 Error Page with Product Suggestions

## Overview
Transform the basic 404 page into a helpful landing page with search functionality and product recommendations, recently viewed and whislist.


## Rationale

The current 404 page is minimal - just a large '404', brief text, and a home link. Users who land on broken links are high-intent visitors who deserve a better recovery path. An enhanced 404 can recover these potential customers.
